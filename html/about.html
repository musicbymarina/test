About Marina

My name is Marina, a girl full of contradictions : born in Marseilles, the city of French hip hop, the week when Saturday Night Fever was released, I love a good pogo brawl at rock concerts. I've been living in Paris since 2001, and I've been clubbing for 25 years.

After my first music blog musicbymarina.fr was created in 2011, I started following djs, producers and rock bands throughout Europe with my camera. My first cam was a Sony compact in 2012, then came shortly my current Nikon reflex camera, plain, without using any extra features such as flash, lightings, nor image editing softwares. I just press the shutter button in order to not denature my own vision.

I was lucky to catch the eye of some cool people and since then I have been commissioned photo reports and portraits backstage by various magazines and musical labels.

I try to not stage my photos, except for some mirror portraits backstage : the reflection of the musicians reveals their personnality : those who live with their narcissism, those who play with it, and those who have trouble taking a look in the mirror.

I like to transpose in my pictures the emotional link between the musician and his audience, and be on stage so that my photos show faithfully what the musician feels when I press the shutter button.

Exhibitions

2017:

After launching shows in France for the past two year, with a focus on electronic music producers , my last permanent exhibition is held in Berlin, at the Beatport offices, from the 29th of September, 2017.
This permanent exhibition, by appointment only, represents electronic djs and producers I have taken since 2012. The content of the exhibition is updated with new portraits and pictures every year.

List of electronic artists from the exhibition (first serie) : 2manydjs, Andrew Weatherall, Azari & III, Black Coffee, Catz n Dogz, Charlotte de Witte, Daniel Avery, DJ Harvey, DJ Hell, Erol Alkan, Maceo Plex, Miss Kittin, Nina Kraviz, Optimo, Ricardo Villalobos, Richie Hawtin, Roman Fluegel.
